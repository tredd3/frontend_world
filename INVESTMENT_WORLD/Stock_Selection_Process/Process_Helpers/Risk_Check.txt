
--------------------------------- RISKS --------------------------------------
---Business Risk:
Also check RHP/DRHP to see the risks mentioned by the management
competetive risk - threat from existing players or new entrants 
pricing power risk
sector innovation risk (EV,5G)
less market size
substitution risk - mainly for chemical/technology
low cost imports risk
legal/scams Risk
regulatory risk/govt policy risk - power sector(controlling prices),allow imports without anti-dumping duty(chemicals,textiles)
business becoming commodity type(no value addition hence products can be easily copied) - you will lose margins and pricing power


---Financial Risk:
high Debt - can lead to bankruptcy during bear cycle
continous upgradation capex - low returns for shareholders
high spend on R&D - can lead to bankruptcy

---Management Risk
old management, with no succession plan
fraud - related party transactions to the companies in which management/promoters have interest 
not focusing on core business, trying to diworsify
not focusing on share holders
irrelevant skills / experience

---Investment Returns Risk:
1)Loss of Capital
2)Not getting Adequate returns(oppurtunity cost) - if u invest ur money in a given company u cannot invest in other companies
 so make sure that u pick the best companies possible/u think will become best over the years
3)are people willing to buy the stock for future and take the stock price higher?


--------------- How to reduce risk --------------
Think for some time with a cool and calm mind and you will get all the scenarios - don't predict about macroeconomy because over 
 the years it will and it has to improve.
Keep reading and updating knowledge everyday - you might not invest everyyear but u need to be 
   updated which reduces ur risk to  a significant amount
Review the stock performance and company performance anually
